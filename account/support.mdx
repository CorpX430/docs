KitaFund SPCX Market includes a built-in live support chat so you can get help without leaving the platform. When you open Support, you are connected to an SPCX support agent who can answer questions about your account, walk you through a deposit, check on an order, and more. The support panel displays **Live Support ONLINE** with a welcome message confirming that an agent is available.

## How to Access Support

You can reach Support from anywhere in the app:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sidebar">
    Tap the menu icon in the top-left corner of any page to open the sidebar navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Support">
    Select **Support** from the navigation list. The live chat interface opens with a welcome message from your support agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start your conversation">
    Type your question in the message field and tap **Send**, or use one of the quick-action buttons to jump straight to a common topic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Support Can Help With

Your support agent can assist you with a wide range of topics, including:

- **Account questions** — profile details, investor status, and access issues
- **Deposit issues** — how to fund your account, deposit status, and payment troubleshooting
- **Order status** — checking where your SPCX orders stand
- **Platform navigation** — finding features, understanding your dashboard, and general how-to questions

## Quick Action Buttons

Three quick-action buttons appear above the chat input so you can start a conversation on a common topic with a single tap:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Account" icon="user">
    Ask about your profile, investor status, or sign-in issues.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Orders" icon="clipboard-list">
    Check on an existing order or ask how the order process works.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments" icon="credit-card">
    Get help with a deposit, payment method, or transaction question.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  When an agent is ready, the **Live Support ONLINE** badge appears at the top of the chat panel alongside your agent's welcome message.
</Note>

## Tips for Getting Faster Help

A few simple steps can speed up your support conversation significantly:

- **Search the documentation first.** Many common questions — how to deposit, how to sign out, where to find your order history — are answered in these docs. Checking here first may save you time.
- **Include your email address and order ID** when asking about a specific deposit or transaction. This lets the agent locate your record immediately.
- **Use the Payments quick button** for urgent payment or deposit issues — it routes your message to a payment specialist straight away.
- **Be specific about what you are trying to do.** For example, instead of "my account isn't working," describe the exact step where you ran into a problem.

<Tip>
  The support agent can answer common platform questions instantly — including how to make a deposit, how to sign out, and where to find your account information. Try typing your question directly and you will often get an immediate response.
</Tip>
