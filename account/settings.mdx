The Settings page is your central hub for managing your KitaFund SPCX Market account. From here you can review your profile details, control which email notifications you receive, configure passkey sign-in, and end your session whenever needed. Open Settings at any time by tapping **Settings** in the sidebar navigation.

## Profile

Your profile section displays the name and email address associated with your SPCX investor account.

- **Display Name** — the name shown across the platform. Tap **Edit** next to your name to update it, then confirm the change.
- **Email Address** — shown as read-only. Your registered email cannot be changed directly from Settings.
- **Investor Status** — reflects your current access level: *Pending*, *Approved*, or *Access Denied*.

<Note>
  Your email address is locked after registration. If you need to update it, contact SPCX Support through the **Support** page and an agent will assist you.
</Note>

## Passkey

You can register a passkey to sign in with biometrics or your device PIN instead of using an email link. Passkeys are faster and more secure than traditional sign-in methods.

To set one up, see the dedicated guide:

- [Set Up Passkey Sign-In](/account/passkey)

## Notifications

The Notifications section lets you choose which email updates SPCX sends you. Toggle each option on or off to match your preferences.

| Notification | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Email Updates** | Company announcements and regulatory filings |
| **Price Alerts** | A notification when SPCX moves ±5% |
| **Portfolio Updates** | Changes to your holdings and account balance |
| **Weekly Digest** | A SpaceX news roundup delivered every Monday |

Your preferences are saved automatically as soon as you toggle each switch — there is no separate Save button.

## Security

### Signing Out

You can sign out of SPCX Market in two ways:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign out from the sidebar">
    Open the sidebar by tapping the menu icon in the top-left corner of any page. Scroll to the bottom of the sidebar and tap **Sign Out**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign out from Settings">
    Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page and tap the **Sign Out** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Signing out ends your current session and returns you to the sign-in screen. Any unsaved draft changes to your display name will be discarded.

### Session Management

SPCX Market keeps you signed in on your device until you explicitly sign out. If you are using a shared or public device, always sign out after each session to protect your account.
