The SPCX Market leadership team brings together the founders and senior executives who built SpaceX from the ground up. On the Management page of the platform at [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live), you can explore each leader's profile, access their full biography through the **LEARN MORE** button on their listing, and review the corporate governance documents that underpin the company's investor commitments.

## Executive Leadership

## Committee Charters

The Management section of the SPCX Market platform includes downloadable committee charter documents for investor review. These governance documents outline the mandate, composition, and responsibilities of SpaceX's board-level committees:

- **Audit Committee Charter** — Defines the scope of financial oversight, audit independence, and risk management responsibilities.
- **Compensation and Nominating Committee Charter** — Covers executive compensation frameworks, director nomination procedures, and related governance policies.

You can access both charters directly from the bottom of the Management page by tapping the document link next to each charter name.

<Note>
  Full executive biographies and the complete suite of committee charter documents are available in the **Management** section of the SPCX Market platform at [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live). Tap **LEARN MORE** next to any leader's name to view their full profile, or tap a charter link to open the corresponding governance document.
</Note>
