## Finding Announcements in the App

All official communications appear in the **Investor Updates** tab of the SPCX Market mobile app. To access them:

1. Open the SPCX Market app and sign in to your account at [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live).
2. Tap **Investor Updates** in the bottom navigation or side menu.
3. Scroll through the feed to review announcements in reverse chronological order — the most recent update always appears at the top.
4. Tap any announcement card to read the full release and access supporting documents.

## Contacting the Investor Relations Team

If you have questions about a specific announcement or need clarification on an offering, use the **CONTACT US** button located on the Investor Updates page. Tapping this button opens a direct line to the SPCX Market investor relations team, who can address questions about timing, eligibility, and participation for any listed offering or disclosure.

  All announcements are official communications from SPCX Market, inc. and are published exclusively in the **Investor Updates** section of the platform at [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live).
