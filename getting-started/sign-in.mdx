Once your investor access request has been approved and your account is active, signing in to SPCX Market takes just a few seconds. The platform identifies you by your registered email address — no password to remember. You can also set up a Passkey for even faster, biometric-based sign-in from your mobile device.

## Sign in with your email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the sign-in page">
    Go to [spcxipo.live/signin](https://spcxipo.live/signin) in your browser. You can also reach it from the home page by tapping the **Sign In** icon in the top-right corner of the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your registered email address">
    Type the email address you used when you submitted your access request into the **Email Address** field. The field is not case-sensitive, but make sure there are no extra spaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Continue">
    Tap the **Continue** button. The platform looks up your account status in real time. This usually takes less than a second.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're taken to your dashboard">
    If your account is active and approved, you are immediately redirected to your investor dashboard. From here you can view live SPCX market data, manage deposits, and place orders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign in with a Passkey

Passkey sign-in lets you authenticate using Face ID, Touch ID, or your device PIN instead of typing your email every time. It's faster, more secure, and works entirely on your device.

To enable Passkey sign-in, go to **Settings** inside your investor dashboard and follow the steps to register a Passkey for your account. Once set up, your device will offer Passkey authentication automatically the next time you visit the sign-in page.

<Note>
  If you sign in and are redirected to an **Access Pending** screen, your access request is still under admin review. You will receive an email notification as soon as your account is activated — no action is required on your part in the meantime.
</Note>

<Warning>
  If you do not yet have an account, signing in will return a "no account found" error. Use the **Request Access** link on the sign-in page — or go directly to [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live) — to submit your investor access request first.
</Warning>
