Once your investor access request has been approved and your account is active, signing in to SPCX Market takes just a few seconds. The platform identifies you by your registered email address — no password to remember.

## Sign in with your email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the sign-in page">
    Go to [spcxipo.live/signin](https://spcxipo.live/signin) in your browser. You can also reach it from the home page by tapping the **Sign In** icon in the top-right corner of the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your registered email address">
    Type the email address you used when you submitted your access request into the **Email Address** field. The field is not case-sensitive, but make sure there are no extra spaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Continue">
    Tap the **Continue** button. The platform looks up your account status in real time. This usually takes less than a second.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're taken to your dashboard">
    If your account is active and approved, you are immediately redirected to your investor dashboard. From here you can view live SPCX market data, manage deposits, and place orders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you sign in and are redirected to an **Access Pending** screen, your access request is still under review. You will receive an email notification as soon as your account is activated — no action is required on your part in the meantime.
</Note>

<Warning>
  If you do not yet have an account, signing in will return a "no account found" error. Use the **Request Access** link on the sign-in page — or go directly to [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live) — to submit your investor access request first.
</Warning>
