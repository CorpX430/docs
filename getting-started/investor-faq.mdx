Whether you're exploring SPCX Market for the first time or you've just been approved and are ready to get started, the answers below cover the questions investors ask most often. If you don't find what you're looking for here, reach out to the investor relations team directly at [ir@spcxipo.live](mailto:ir@spcxipo.live).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is SPCX?">
    SPCX is the ticker symbol for SpaceX shares on the KitaFund SPCX Market platform. SPCX trades on NASDAQ and represents equity in SpaceX — one of the most anticipated IPOs in the history of the technology and aerospace sectors. SPCX Market gives individual investors structured access to SpaceX shares.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who is eligible to participate?">
    Anyone can submit an access request, but account activation requires approval by the KitaFund team. During the review process, the team verifies eligibility criteria including accredited investor status in supported jurisdictions. You do not need to provide documentation at the request stage — the team will contact you if additional information is needed during review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I place an order?">
    Once your account is active and funded, navigate to the trading section of your dashboard. You can submit a **market order** to buy or sell SPCX at the current price, or a **limit order** to specify the exact price at which you want your order to execute. Orders are confirmed on screen and visible in your order history immediately after submission.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the minimum investment?">
    The minimum IPO buy-in on SPCX Market is **$1,500**. You must have at least this amount deposited and available in your account before you can place your first SPCX order. There is no stated maximum, though large orders may be subject to additional review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which cryptocurrencies are accepted for deposits?">
    SPCX Market accepts deposits in supported cryptocurrencies. The full list of accepted assets is shown on the **Deposit** screen inside your investor dashboard, where you'll also find your unique deposit address and current network options. Deposits are credited once the required number of on-chain confirmations is reached.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How are my shares tracked?">
    Your SPCX holdings are displayed in real time on your **Holdings** dashboard. You can see your total share count, average cost basis, current market value, and unrealised gain or loss at a glance. All positions are held in segregated custody, and your account history provides a full audit trail of every order and deposit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    The KitaFund investor relations team is available by email at [ir@spcxipo.live](mailto:ir@spcxipo.live). Include your registered email address and a brief description of your issue so the team can assist you as quickly as possible. For urgent trading or account matters, note your account status and order reference numbers when you write in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. Your account information is stored securely, and all sessions are authenticated before any account data is returned. All account holdings are held in segregated custody, and standard regulatory protections apply to eligible accounts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
