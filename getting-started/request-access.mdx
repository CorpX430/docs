SPCX Market uses a gated onboarding process to ensure every investor account is individually reviewed before activation. You start by submitting a short request — just your name and email — and the KitaFund team personally evaluates each application. Once your request is approved, you receive an email with instructions to complete your account setup and sign in for the first time.

## How to submit your access request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to spcxipo.live">
    Open [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live) in your browser or on mobile. You'll land on the SPCX Market home page, which displays the current SPCX trading status and the investor sign-up form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to the sign-up form">
    Scroll past the hero section to reach the **Sign up for SpaceX Investor Access** form. You can also tap any **Sign Up** call-to-action on the page to jump directly to it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your full name and email address">
    Type your **Full Name** and **Email Address** into the respective fields. Use an email address you check regularly — your approval notification will be sent there. Both fields are required; you'll see an inline error if either is left blank or the email format is invalid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Submit">
    Tap the **Submit** button. If your submission is successful, the page redirects to an **Access Pending** screen confirming that your request has been received. You'll see your email address displayed on screen as confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after you submit

After you submit, your request enters the KitaFund admin review queue. The team personally reviews every application — you can expect a response within **48 hours**. When your request is approved, you'll receive an email at the address you provided with a link to complete your account setup. From there you can sign in to your dashboard, deposit funds, and begin trading SPCX.

If you submitted using the wrong email address, navigate back to the home page and resubmit with the correct one.

<Note>
  Anyone can apply for SPCX investor access — there is no pre-screening before you submit. All eligibility checks are performed during the admin review stage. If your request is not approved, you will be notified by email.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is SPCX?">
    SPCX is the ticker symbol for SpaceX shares on the KitaFund SPCX Market platform. It is the first regulated exchange dedicated to pre-IPO space-economy equities, giving individual investors the ability to buy and sell SpaceX shares ahead of the public listing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who is eligible to apply?">
    Anyone can submit an access request. The KitaFund admin team reviews each application and verifies eligibility — including accredited investor status in supported jurisdictions — before activating an account. You do not need to provide verification documents at the request stage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does the approval process take?">
    The team aims to review all requests within **48 hours** of submission. If you haven't received an email after 48 hours, check your spam or junk folder first, then contact investor relations at [ir@spcxipo.live](mailto:ir@spcxipo.live).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens after I'm approved?">
    Once approved, you'll receive an email notification with instructions to activate your account. Sign in at [spcxipo.live/signin](https://spcxipo.live/signin) using the email address you registered with, deposit a minimum of **$1,500** to meet the IPO buy-in requirement, and you're ready to place your first SPCX order.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
