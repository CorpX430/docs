KitaFund SPCX Market is a mobile-first platform that gives individual investors access to SpaceX IPO shares, trading under the ticker **SPCX** on NASDAQ. Rather than waiting for a traditional brokerage allocation, you apply directly through [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live), get reviewed by the KitaFund team, and — once approved — can deposit funds and start building your SPCX position.

## Who is SPCX Market for?

SPCX Market is built for individual investors who want direct, early access to SpaceX equity. Whether you're a first-time IPO participant or an experienced investor adding a space-economy position to your portfolio, the platform guides you through every step — from your initial access request all the way through to tracking your holdings.

## How the platform works

The investor journey follows four straightforward stages:

1. **Request access** — Submit your name and email at [spcxipo.live](https://spcxipo.live). Your request goes to the KitaFund admin team for review.
2. **Approval & account activation** — Once approved, you receive an email notification to set up your account and sign in.
3. **Deposit funds** — Fund your account using supported cryptocurrencies to meet the minimum buy-in requirement.
4. **Trade SPCX** — Place market or limit orders for SPCX shares and monitor your holdings directly in the app.
