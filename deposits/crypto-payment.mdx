Funding your KitaFund SPCX Market account is done entirely through cryptocurrency — there are no card or bank transfer options. The process takes just a few minutes: you choose how much to deposit, select your coin, send the funds from your external wallet, and confirm the payment in the app. The SPCX platform team then reviews your deposit and credits your shares. The minimum deposit amount is **$1,500**.

## Deposit Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Payment section from your dashboard">
    Open the SPCX Market app and go to the **Orders** screen. If you have no existing holdings, you'll see a **Deposit Funds** button directly on your dashboard — tap it to go straight to the payment flow. Otherwise, navigate to **Orders** from the side menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your cryptocurrency">
    Choose the coin you want to use for this deposit: **BTC** (Bitcoin), **ETH** (Ethereum), or **DOGE** (Dogecoin). Make sure you have sufficient funds in your external wallet for the coin you select. Each coin has its own unique wallet address — your selection determines which address you'll be given.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the wallet address shown on screen">
    The app will display the current deposit wallet address for your selected coin. Tap the **Copy** button to copy it exactly. Do not type the address manually — even a single character error will send your funds to an unreachable destination.

    <Tip>
      If you need to verify the address you used after a transaction is sent, retrieve your transaction hash (TXID) from your external wallet and use a blockchain explorer to confirm the destination. Do not rely on screenshots or saved addresses — always copy the address fresh from the app for each new deposit.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your crypto from your external wallet">
    Open your external crypto wallet or exchange app and initiate a withdrawal or transfer to the address you copied. Send at least the equivalent of **$1,500 USD** in your chosen cryptocurrency. Double-check that:
    - The destination address matches exactly what the app displayed.
    - You are sending the **correct coin** to the address (e.g., BTC only to the BTC address).
    - Network fees are accounted for — the received amount after fees must still meet the minimum.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to the app and tap 'Done' to confirm your payment">
    Once you've sent the crypto from your external wallet, return to the SPCX Market app and tap the **Done** button on the payment screen. This records your deposit in the system and notifies the platform team that a transfer is on its way. You do not need to wait for on-chain confirmation before tapping Done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for platform confirmation — check your deposit status in Orders">
    After you tap Done, your deposit appears in the **Orders** screen with a status of **Pending**. The SPCX platform team will review the deposit once your transaction is confirmed on the blockchain and update the status to **Completed** when your shares are credited. See [Understanding Your Deposit Status](./deposit-status) for details on what each status means.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Only send the selected cryptocurrency to its designated address. For example, send **only BTC** to the Bitcoin address, and **only ETH** to the Ethereum address. Sending the wrong coin to an address — such as sending ETH to a BTC address — will result in **permanent, unrecoverable loss of funds**. The SPCX team cannot retrieve cross-chain transfers.
</Warning>

## Supported Coins

SPCX Market currently accepts deposits in the following three cryptocurrencies. All three are treated equally in terms of the deposit process — only the wallet address differs for each.

| Coin | Symbol | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bitcoin | BTC | The most widely supported digital asset; confirmation times vary by network congestion. |
| Ethereum | ETH | Fast confirmations; broadly available on exchanges and wallets worldwide. |
| Dogecoin | DOGE | Low fees and fast block times; a straightforward option for DOGE holders. |

Regardless of which coin you use, the deposit value is evaluated in USD equivalent at the time of review by the platform team. All three coins must meet the **$1,500 minimum** in USD value at the time of sending.
