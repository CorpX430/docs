KitaFund SPCX Market lets you fund your account and participate in SpaceX IPO share access entirely through cryptocurrency. There are no card or bank transfer options — all deposits are made via crypto, keeping the process fast, borderless, and secure. Once your deposit is received and reviewed by the SPCX team, shares are credited to your account so you can start building your position.

## Minimum Deposit

The minimum deposit amount is **$1,500**, which corresponds to the minimum IPO buy-in on the platform. You may deposit more than $1,500 in a single transaction, and you can make multiple deposits over time to increase your position.

## Supported Cryptocurrencies

SPCX Market currently accepts deposits in three cryptocurrencies. Each coin has its own dedicated wallet address — make sure you send only the matching coin to each address.

## How the Deposit Flow Works

Making a deposit takes just a few steps inside the app. For the full step-by-step walkthrough, see the [Crypto Payment guide](./crypto-payment).

1. Open the **Orders** screen and tap **Deposit Funds**.
2. Enter the amount you want to deposit (minimum $1,500).
3. Select your preferred cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, or DOGE).
4. Copy the wallet address shown and send your crypto from your external wallet.
5. Tap **Done** inside the app to record your deposit.
6. The SPCX team reviews your deposit and credits your shares.

<Note>
  Shares are not credited automatically. After your crypto transaction is confirmed on-chain, the SPCX platform team manually reviews your deposit and credits the corresponding shares to your account. Processing times may vary.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Always copy the wallet address directly from the app at the time of your deposit. Wallet addresses are managed by the platform team and **may change without notice**. Never reuse an address from a previous session, a screenshot, or a third-party source — sending funds to an outdated address may result in permanent loss.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long do deposits take?">
    Processing time has two stages. First, your crypto transaction needs to be confirmed on the blockchain — this typically takes anywhere from a few minutes (ETH, DOGE) to up to an hour or more (BTC) depending on network congestion. Once the on-chain confirmation is complete, the SPCX platform team manually reviews and approves your deposit before crediting your shares. Total processing time is usually within one business day, but may vary. You can monitor your deposit status on the **Orders** screen at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I send the wrong amount?">
    If you send less than the $1,500 minimum, your deposit may not be processed and shares will not be credited. If this happens, contact SPCX support as soon as possible with your transaction hash and deposit details so the team can investigate. Sending more than your intended amount is generally fine — the team will credit shares based on the value received, but reach out to support if you have any concerns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I deposit multiple times?">
    Yes. You can make as many separate deposits as you like to build up your position over time. Each deposit is recorded individually, and shares are credited after each one is reviewed and confirmed by the platform team. There is no cap on the total number of deposits per account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
