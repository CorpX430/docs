Every deposit you make on KitaFund SPCX Market moves through a short lifecycle before your shares are credited. The platform team reviews each deposit manually after it is confirmed on the blockchain, which means your deposit status is your real-time window into where things stand. You can check the status of any deposit at any time from the **Orders** screen in the app.

## Deposit Statuses

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Pending" icon="clock">
    Your deposit has been submitted and recorded. The SPCX team is waiting for blockchain confirmation of your transaction and will then begin their manual review. No action is required from you at this stage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Completed" icon="circle-check">
    Your deposit has been confirmed and reviewed. Shares have been, or are in the process of being, credited to your account. You should see your holdings update on the dashboard shortly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Failed" icon="circle-xmark">
    Your deposit could not be processed. This may be due to an insufficient amount, an unrecognized transaction, or a platform issue. Contact SPCX support immediately with your deposit details.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Deposits are confirmed and credited manually by the SPCX platform team — there is no automatic on-chain processing. Processing times may vary depending on blockchain network speed and team review queue. Most deposits are resolved within one business day.
</Note>

## How to Check Your Deposit Status

1. Open the SPCX Market app and tap the **menu icon** in the top-left corner of the dashboard.
2. Navigate to the **Orders** screen.
3. Locate your deposit in the list — each entry shows the amount, coin, date, and current status.
4. The status label (**Pending**, **Completed**, or **Failed**) updates in real time as the platform team processes your deposit.

You do not need to refresh or contact support just to check a status — the Orders screen reflects the latest state of every deposit on your account.

## What to Do If Your Deposit Is Stuck in Pending

A deposit in **Pending** status is normal and expected for the first several hours after you send your crypto. However, if your deposit has been Pending for more than **24 hours**, consider the following steps:

1. **Verify your transaction on-chain.** Use a blockchain explorer (such as [mempool.space](https://mempool.space) for BTC or [etherscan.io](https://etherscan.io) for ETH) to confirm that your transaction was broadcast and has received sufficient confirmations.
2. **Confirm you tapped 'Done' in the app.** If you sent the crypto but closed the app before tapping Done, the platform team may not have been notified. Contact support with your transaction hash to resolve this.
3. **Check that you met the minimum.** Deposits below the **$1,500 USD equivalent** minimum may be held or rejected during review.
4. **Contact support.** If everything looks correct on your end, reach out to the SPCX support team (see below) with your registered email address, the deposit amount, the coin used, and your blockchain transaction hash (TXID).

## What to Do If Your Deposit Shows as Failed

A **Failed** status means the platform team was unable to process your deposit. Common reasons include:

- The received amount did not meet the $1,500 minimum after network fees.
- The transaction was not detected on-chain within the expected window.
- There was a mismatch between the deposit record and the blockchain transaction.

If your deposit has failed, do not resend funds before speaking with support — the team needs to investigate the original transaction first.

## Contact Support

If you have questions about a deposit in any status, reach out to the SPCX support team and include the following in your message:

- Your registered email address
- The deposit amount and coin (BTC, ETH, or DOGE)
- The date and time you sent the transaction
- Your blockchain transaction hash (TXID), if available

Contact support at: **support@kitafund.com**
