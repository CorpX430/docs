## Price Chart

The interactive area chart displays SPCX's price history so you can see how the stock has moved over time. Use the time-period selector above the chart to zoom in or out.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Time Periods">
    | Period | What It Shows |
    |---|---|
    | **Live** | Real-time intraday feed with a pulsing green indicator |
    | **1D** | Last trading day in 5-minute intervals |
    | **1W** | Past 7 days in hourly intervals |
    | **1M** | Past 30 days in 6-hour intervals |
    | **3M** | Past 90 days in daily intervals |
    | **1Y** | Past 252 trading days in daily intervals |
    | **5Y** | Past 5 years in weekly intervals |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Tap or click any period button to instantly reload the chart for that timeframe. The **Live** button shows a pulsing indicator to confirm you are viewing real-time data.

## Your Holdings

At the bottom of the dashboard, a dedicated Holdings card shows you a live summary of your investment position.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Shares Owned" icon="chart-pie">
    The total number of SPCX shares credited to your account, displayed to four decimal places.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Market Value" icon="dollar-sign">
    Your shares multiplied by the current live price, giving you an up-to-the-minute portfolio value in USD.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

If you do not yet own any shares, a **Deposit Funds** button appears in the Holdings card, taking you directly to the deposit flow to purchase your first shares.

<Tip>
  The dashboard refreshes price data and your portfolio value every 30 seconds automatically. Pull down to manually refresh on mobile, or reload the page on desktop, to fetch the very latest quote.
</Tip>
