Your Holdings summary gives you a clear, up-to-date view of your SpaceX IPO (SPCX) position. Every time a deposit is confirmed by the KitaFund SPCX Market platform team, your share count and portfolio value update automatically so you always know where you stand.

## What Your Holdings Display

Once you own shares, your Holdings card — visible both on the main dashboard and the dedicated Holdings view — shows three key figures:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Shares Owned" icon="layer-group">
    The total number of SPCX shares credited to your account after confirmed deposits, shown to four decimal places.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Average Cost Per Share" icon="calculator">
    The weighted average price you paid across all your purchases, displayed in USD.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Current Market Value" icon="trending-up">
    Your share count multiplied by the live SPCX price, recalculated every 30 seconds.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How Shares Are Credited

Shares are not added to your account the moment you submit a deposit. The platform team reviews and confirms each deposit before your holdings are updated. Here is the full sequence:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Your Deposit">
    Complete the deposit flow — choosing your share quantity and payment method — and submit your order. You will see a **Payment Submitted** confirmation screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Platform Review">
    The KitaFund SPCX Market team reviews your deposit. During this time, your order status shows as **Pending** on the Orders screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation and Credit">
    Once confirmed, your share count is updated and your Holdings immediately reflect the new position. Your order status changes to **Completed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Holdings update only when your deposit is confirmed by the platform team. Submitting a deposit does not instantly add shares to your account. Check the [Orders](/platform/orders) screen to monitor the status of any pending deposits.
</Note>

## How Average Cost Is Calculated

Your average cost per share is a **weighted average** across all your confirmed purchases. Each time a new deposit is confirmed, the platform recalculates your average cost using the total amount paid divided by the total shares received.

For example, if you buy 10 shares at $1,500 each and later buy 5 more shares at $1,000 each, your average cost is:

> (10 × $1,500 + 5 × $1,000) ÷ 15 shares = **$1,333.33 per share**

This means early purchases at a higher price are offset proportionally by later purchases at a lower price, giving you a true blended cost basis.
