The Orders screen gives you a complete record of every deposit you have submitted on KitaFund SPCX Market. Whether you are waiting for a payment to clear or reviewing past transactions, this is the place to check the current state of your funds before they become shares.

## What the Orders Screen Shows

Each entry in your orders list displays the key details of a single deposit transaction:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Amount and Shares" icon="receipt">
    The total USD amount of the deposit and the number of SPCX shares it is intended to purchase at the price locked in at submission.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment Method" icon="credit-card">
    Whether the deposit was submitted via debit/credit card or a crypto wallet (BTC, ETH, or DOGE), along with the selected coin where applicable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Submission Date" icon="calendar">
    The timestamp of when you submitted the deposit order so you can track how long each review takes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Order Status" icon="circle-dot">
    A live status badge showing whether your deposit is Pending, Completed, or Failed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Order Statuses

Every deposit moves through one of the following statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Your deposit has been submitted and is awaiting review and confirmation by the KitaFund SPCX Market platform team. Your shares have not yet been credited. |
| **Completed** | The platform team has confirmed your deposit. Your SPCX shares have been credited to your account and your Holdings have been updated. |
| **Failed** | Your deposit could not be processed. This may happen if a payment could not be verified. Contact support if you believe this is an error. |

## Submitting a New Deposit Order

You can start a new deposit at any time directly from the Orders screen or from the Holdings card on your dashboard. The deposit flow walks you through selecting your share quantity, choosing a payment method, and submitting your funds.

For a full walkthrough of the deposit process, see [Deposits Overview](/deposits/overview).

<Note>
  Deposit confirmations are processed by the platform team during business hours. Processing times may vary. You will see your order status update from **Pending** to **Completed** once the team has reviewed and approved your payment.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Orders cannot be cancelled once submitted. Before you confirm your deposit, double-check the share quantity, total amount, and payment method on the review screen. If you have concerns about a submitted order, please contact the SPCX Market support team directly.
</Warning>
