## Reading the Full Details of an Update

Each announcement card in the feed includes a **LEARN MORE** button. Tapping it opens the complete text of that announcement — including financial figures, dates, regulatory context, and any action items relevant to you as an investor. You can return to the main feed at any time by navigating back.
