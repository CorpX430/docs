## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Submit your name and email on the sign-up page. Your request goes to the SPCX team for review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved">
    Once approved, you receive an email confirmation with a link to create and activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your account">
    Deposit a minimum of **$1,500** using BTC, ETH, or DOGE to purchase your first SPCX shares.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    Monitor live price data, review your holdings, and stay informed with real-time investor updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
